## Bridge Inspection Ontology (BrIO)

## Description
The Bridge Inspection Ontology (BrIO) is designed to represent knowledge about bridge inspection planning and bridge inspection data management. The ontology provides a standardized vocabulary for describing concepts, entities, and relationships relevant to bridge inspections. BrIO extends OCQA with bridge-specific concepts and supports structured information exchange in digital twins for bridge maintenance and bridge management.
