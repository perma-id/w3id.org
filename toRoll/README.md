# toRoll

This [W3ID](https://w3id.org) provides a persistent URI namespace for a vocabulary of variations of otijjot meshunnot (special letters) and Hebrew letters decorated with tagin (strokes). The vocabulary was developed and maintained within the Joint Research and Development project "toRoll - Materialized Holiness" funded by the Federal Ministry of Research, Technology and Space (01UL2202B) in 2022-2026.
