This space is functional to host the ontologies developed by Fondazione Zeri (University of Bologna). This cultural institution hosts the photo archive of the Italian connoisseur Federico Zeri and supports projects in the field of digital art history and ontology engineering to expose their datasets (main topic: art history, art market studies) as Linked Open Data.
