Abstract

The purpose of this vocabulary is to describe in a semantic manner information related to university Administrative and Service Staff (PAS).

Introduction back to ToC

This vocabulary represents information related to university Administrative and Service Staff (PAS). The data used in it comes from a set of public spanish universities, more specifically, they belong to: the Automonous University of Madrid (UAM), the Carlos III University of Madrid (UC3M), the Complutense University of Madrid (UCM), the King Juan Carlos University (URJC), the University of Valladolid (UVa) and the University of Huelva (UHU). In this way, this vocabulary describes information about the PAS, which works in a university as well as provides services to another unit.

Moreover, it uses the geo and geosparql vocabularies to represent the geographical location of the university where the PAS carries out his work, according to different coordinate reference systems commonly used for this type of data. Additionally, it reuses terms from the schema.org vocabulary for the representation of concepts and relations that are used to describe people. Finally, it also reuses the organizations vocabulary to represent the university entity, which gathers all the Administrative and Service Staff (PAS) that work in it.

Vocabulary for the representation of university Administrative and Service Staff (PAS): Description back to ToC

Figure 1 shows the classes and properties of the vocabulary that represents the data of university Administrative and Service Staff (PAS):

As mentioned before, the scope of this vocabulary is the representation of data from university Administrative and Service Staff (PAS). To accomplish this purpose, as it can be seen in the figure, several classes and properties have been defined in order to describe these data, such as the PersonalAdministracionServicios class, which collects information about his date of birth, his year of incorporation, if the PAS provides services to another unit or not, his number of weekly hours with a part-time job, if his job is paid or not, if he teaches lessons or not and his number of three year periods. Furthermore, the PersonalAdministracionServicios class has several SKOS concept schemes to represent his gender, his dedication, his contract type, the degree it is required, the service provided, the staff type, the scale corp PAS and his administrative situation. Besides, it is described for each PAS his nationality country (which is also represented by the vocabulary geosparql), continent and aggregation of countries, and also his residence municipality (which is also represented by the vocabulary geosparql), province and autonomous community. Regarding the other classes, the PAS works in a university during a given year, as well as he provides services to another unit. Additionally, it has been reused the vocabulary schema:Person to describe the PAS. Finally, it has been also reused the vocabulary of org:Organization to show how the class Universidad has a geographical location, represented by the vocabulary geosparql.
