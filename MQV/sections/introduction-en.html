Introduction back to ToC

The Mapping Quality Vocabulary (MQV) is defined in OWL2 Web Ontology Language. This document describes the set of classes, properties, and restrictions that constitute the Mapping Quality Vocabulary. This vocabulary specification provides the foundation to implement the capturing of provenance and metadata relating to the creation, quality assessment and quality refinement of mapping definitions. This vocabulary reuses existing vocabularies such as the PROV Ontology (PROV -O) [PROV-O] and the Data Quality Vocabulary (DQV) [DQV], providing additional trustworthiness to the vocabulary.
