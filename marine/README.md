# Semantic assets for the MAREGRAPH project

## About

Persistent identifiers and redirection rules for semantic assets related to marine and aquatic biodiversity, developed within the [MAREGRAPH project](https://www.maregraph.eu/) and in related initiatives.

The purpose of this initiative is to provide stable, resolvable URIs and links for ontologies, controlled vocabularies, data catalogues, SHACL shapes and other semantic artefacts used to describe marine and aquatic biodiversity data.

The namespace is maintained under the control and stewardship of the [Flanders Marine Institute](https://www.vliz.be) (VLIZ) and the [Institute of Cognitive Sciences and Technologies](https://www.istc.cnr.it/en) (CNR-ISTC) of the [National Research Council of Italy](https://www.cnr.it) (CNR).
