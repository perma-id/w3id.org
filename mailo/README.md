# Medical AI Legal Ontology (MAILO)

This is a permanent identifier for the Medical AI Legal Ontology (MAILO),
an OWL 2 / SHACL knowledge graph encoding EU regulatory obligations for
medical AI systems (GDPR, MDR, AI Act, PLD, EHDS, DGA, Data Act, Trade
Secrets Directive), validated against CJEU case law.
