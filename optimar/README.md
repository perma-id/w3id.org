
## Name of the project:

OPTiMAR

---

## Description:
Ontology modeling structural elements, sensors, events, and failures in floating offshore wind platforms, including measurement data, involved actors, and semantic relationships between components. 
Compatible with SOSA for observations and QUDT for units of measure.
