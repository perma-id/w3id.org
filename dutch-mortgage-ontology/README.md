# Dutch Mortgage Ontology

Digital transformation is a business strategy initiative that incorporates digital technology across all areas of an organization, including the business domain, processes, products, employee experience, and customer experience. Large organizations struggle with materializing their digital transformation due to the large amount of complex documents. We propose an ontological approach to digital transformation, aiming to build a semantic infrastructure as the backbone of several services. Our approach relies on the Unified Foundational Ontology (UFO), gUFO (a lightweight implementation of the UFO suitable for Semantic Web OWL 2 DL applications), and the OntoUML modeling language. We prioritize domain understanding through conceptual modeling and Applied Ontology principles, before particular Linked Data implementations. We report preliminary results about how this approach has been adopted in a real-world industry project for the Royal Dutch Association of Civil-law Notaries (KNB: Koninklijke Notariële Beroepsorganisatie), in the Netherlands.
