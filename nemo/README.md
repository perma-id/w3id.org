# Ontology & Conceptual Modeling Research Group (NEMO)

The Ontology & Conceptual Modeling Research Group (NEMO) at the Federal University of Espírito Santo (UFES) (Brazil), investigates the application of domain and foundational ontologies as well as ontology-based techniques in various aspects of conceptual modeling such as information modeling, enterprise modeling, and the Semantic Web.

The w3id.org URLs are used to create permanent URLs to the projects, open-source code and resources of this research group.
