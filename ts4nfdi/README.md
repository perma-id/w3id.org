## Terminology Services 4 NFDI (TS4NFDI)


This W3ID provides a persistent IRI namespace for terminology collections hosted by the [Terminology Services 4 NFDI (TS4NFDI)](https://terminology.services.base4nfdi.de/) project. TS4NFDI is a cross-domain service for the provision, curation, development, harmonization, and mapping of terminologies. It aims to facilitate consensus-building and interoperability of services across disciplines to achieve a shared knowledge representation and knowledge engineering framework. The service seeks to integrate and converge individual solutions into a standardized, interoperable, and sustainable architecture.
