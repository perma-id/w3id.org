Alignment Modules back to ToC

Even though the ODP is motivated by the energy effciency in buildings problem, it is applicable to similar problems from different domains. This section shows the alignments with other ontologies.

DUL Alignment Module back to ToC

This section introduces the alignment of the AffectedBy ODP to the DOLCE+DnS Ultralite (DUL) upper ontology.

The DUL alignment is available at https://raw.githubusercontent.com/iesnaola/AffectedBy/master/alignments/affectedBy_Alignment_DUL.owl

SSN Alignment Module back to ToC

This section introduces the alignment of the AffectedBy ODP to the Semantic Sensor Network (SSN) ontology.

The SSN alignment is available at https://raw.githubusercontent.com/iesnaola/AffectedBy/master/alignments/affectedBy_Alignment_SSN.owl
