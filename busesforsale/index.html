BusesForSale vocabulary

https://w3id.org/busesforsale/

Purpose

This vocabulary defines denormalized first-level properties for BusesForSale schema:BusOrCoach entities. These properties make bus entities easier for bots, retrieval systems, and chunkset embedding pipelines to consume without traversing linked schema.org nodes.

The properties are additive metadata. They do not replace canonical schema.org markup such as schema:offers, schema:vehicleEngine, or schema:additionalProperty.

Machine-readable serializations: Turtle and JSON-LD.

Terms

Brand name. The display name of the brand associated with a bus listing.

For bots: Denormalized from schema:brand/schema:name on a schema:BusOrCoach entity. Expected value: text, for example "Blue Bird".
