
# CNR-ISMAR Persistent Identifiers

This namespace provides persistent identifiers managed by the National Research Council of Italy (CNR), Institute of Marine Sciences (ISMAR).

The purpose of this W3ID namespace is to provide stable, long-term, dereferenceable URIs for semantic resources developed and maintained by CNR-ISMAR, independently from their physical hosting location.

## Namespace

Base URI:

https://w3id.org/cnr-ismar/
